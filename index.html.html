// Who We Are

More than a club —
a startup ecosystem.

The Federation of Entrepreneurship Development (FED) was founded to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world startup building. We nurture, accelerate, and celebrate the next generation of entrepreneurs.

01
Mentorship & Networking

Direct access to 100+ industry experts, founders, and investors from leading organizations.

02
Incubation & Funding

Structured programs to take your idea from whiteboard to market with seed support.

03
Workshops & Competitions

Hands-on learning through hackathons, pitch competitions, and skill-building sessions.

// What We Offer

Programs Built to Launch You

Whether you have an idea or a growing startup, FED has a program tailored to your stage.

🔬

Ideation Lab

A structured 4-week bootcamp to validate your startup idea with real user research and market analysis.

📈

Scale-Up Track

For early-stage startups ready to grow — access mentors, investors, and a 3-month acceleration program.

🎓

Skill Workshops

Weekly hands-on sessions on product design, growth marketing, financial modeling, and pitching.

🤖

Tech for Good

A specialized program for startups solving social or environmental challenges using emerging tech.
